## Investment Thesis (2025–2026)

Menlo is explicitly **"ALL IN on AI"** — their core conviction is that AI is
restructuring every software vertical and the winners will create massive
economic value. Their 2025 State of Generative AI report found enterprise AI
spend hit $37B in 2025, a 3.2x YoY increase.

**Six current focus areas:**
- **AI Infrastructure** — foundation models, tooling, observability
- **Enterprise AI / Vertical AI** — agentic workflows, coding, department-level automation
- **Cybersecurity** — AI-native security, new threat categories created by AI
- **Healthcare** — AI in health is deploying at 2.2x the rate of the broader economy
- **Consumer AI** — intelligence embedded in everyday products and services
- **Fintech** — convergence of previously siloed financial processes into software

**Anthology Fund:** A $100M initiative co-created with Anthropic to fund the next
generation of AI startups.
